How to Make Pork Adobo

Description

Pork Adobo is a popular Filipino dish that consists of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. There are versions that consist of alternative proteins and containing onions, potatoes and eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of pork belly
  • 2 tablespoons of garlic, minced or crushed
  • 5 dried bay leaves
  • 4 tablespoons of vinegar
  • ½ cup of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of peppercorn
  • 2 cups of water
  • salt to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Combine pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic; marinade for 1 hour
  2. Heat the pot and add marinated pork belly and cook for a few minutes
  3. Pour in remaining marinade including garlic
  4. Add water, whole peppercorn, dried bay leaves and bring to a boil
  5. Simmer for 40min to 1 hour
  6. Add vinegar and simmer for 12-15min
  7. Add salt to taste
  8. Serve hot with side of rice or over rice
